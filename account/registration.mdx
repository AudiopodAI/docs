## Creating Your Account

Getting started with AudioPod AI is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your developer account:

### 1. Sign Up

<Card
  title="Create Your AudioPod AI Account"
  icon="user-plus"
  href="https://www.audiopod.ai/auth/signup"
>
  Sign up now and get 10,000 free credits (~$1.33 value) to start building
</Card>

### 2. Account Information

When signing up, you'll need to provide:

- **Email Address**: Used for login and important notifications
- **Full Name**: For account identification and billing
- **Password**: Secure password for your account

<Info>
  The registration process is secure and includes email verification to ensure account safety.
</Info>

### 3. Email Verification

After registration:

1. Check your email for a verification code from AudioPod AI
2. Enter the 6-digit verification code to complete registration
3. Your account will be activated automatically
4. You'll receive your welcome credits (10,000 credits = ~$1.33 value)
5. A free Basic plan subscription will be automatically created for your account

<Info>
  If you don't receive the verification email within 5 minutes, check your spam
  folder or request a new verification code from the registration page. 
  Verification codes expire after 15 minutes for security.
</Info>

## Profile Management

### Updating Your Profile

To update your account information:

1. Go to [Account Settings](https://www.audiopod.ai/dashboard/settings)
2. Click **Profile** in the sidebar
3. Update your information:
   - Full name
   - Company name
   - Phone number (optional)
   - Profile picture

### Email Address Changes

To change your email address:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Email Address**
3. Enter your new email address
4. Verify the new email address
5. Your login credentials will be updated

<Warning>
  Changing your email address will require re-verification. You'll need access
  to both your old and new email addresses to complete the process.
</Warning>

## Security Settings

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA for additional security:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
3. Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
4. Scan the QR code or enter the setup key
5. Enter the verification code to confirm

### Password Management

To change your password:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click **Update Password**

Password requirements:

- Minimum 6 characters
- At least one letter (uppercase or lowercase)
- At least one number

### Login History

Monitor your account security by reviewing login history:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Security**
2. View **Recent Login Activity**
3. Review locations and devices
4. Report any suspicious activity

## Account Verification

### Business Verification

For higher spending limits and enterprise features:

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Verification**
2. Upload business documentation:
   - Business registration certificate
   - Tax ID or VAT number
   - Proof of address
3. Wait for review (typically 1-2 business days)

### Identity Verification

For enhanced security and higher rate limits:

1. Upload a government-issued ID
2. Provide proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
3. Complete verification process

<Info>
  Verification is optional but may be required for certain features or higher
  usage limits.
</Info>

## Account Deletion

If you need to delete your account:

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data, credits, and API
  keys will be permanently removed.
</Warning>

1. Go to **Account Settings** → **Account**
2. Scroll to **Danger Zone**
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Confirm by typing your email address
5. Click **Delete My Account**

Before deletion:

- Download any important data
- Cancel active subscriptions
- Use remaining credits
- Revoke all API keys
